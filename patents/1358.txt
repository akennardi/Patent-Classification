          FIELD OF THE INVENTION
          The present invention relates to a method for preparing an organic intermediate
 5 which belongs to the field of organic synthesis, more particularly, relates to organic
   synthesis intermediates 4-nitro l-1, 8-naphthalic acid synthesis method.
          GENERAL BACKGROUND
          4-nitrol-1, 8-naphthalic acid as an organic synthetic intermediate, it is mainly
10 used for preparing acid dyes and disperse dyes, most of the existing synthesis methods
   are using the process that 5-nitro-acenaphthene is obtained by the nitration of nitric
   acid, then sodium dichromate was oxidized to obtain 4-nitrol-1, 8-naphthalic acid in
   glacial acetic acid medium. This synthesis method requires using nitric acid as reactant,
   the synthesis process is of highly risks, it is use sodium dichromate as oxidant, the
15 environmental pollution is bigger, and the synthesis method is complicated. Therefore,
   it is necessary to put forward a new synthesis method.
          SUMMARY
          Based on the technical problems of the background technology, the purpose of the
20 present invention is to provide organic synthesis intermediates 4-nitro-1, 8-naphthalic
   acid synthesis method, comprises the following steps:
          A: 2, 7-dibromo -1, 8-naphthalate methanol, 1.2 L sodium nitrate solution were
   added to the reaction vessel, the temperature of the solution was raised to 40-50 'C,
   controlled the stirring speed at 230-260 rpm, added benzyl propionate solution,
25 continued to react for 30-50 min;
          B: erbium oxide powder was added to the reaction vessel in batches within 60-90
   min, continued to react for 60-80 min, added oxalic acid solution, adjusted the pH to
   5-6, used bromine phenol solution to extract for multiple times, washed with 4-chlorine
   phenol solution for several times, recrystallized in the chlorobutane solution,
30 dehydrated with dehydration, got the finished product 4 - nitro 1, 8-naphthalene acid.
