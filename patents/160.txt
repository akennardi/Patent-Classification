         FIELD OF THE INVENTION
 5       The present invention relates to a method for preparing a organic synthesis
   intermediates which belongs to the field of organic synthesis, more particularly,
   relates to organic synthesis intermediates, 4, 5-dichloro-1, 8-naphthalic anhydride
   synthesis method.
10       GENERAL BACKGROUND
         4, 5-dichloro-1, 8-naphthalic anhydride is mainly used to produce the dye
   fluorescence red. Most of the existing synthesis methods are using oxidation reaction
   by 4, 5-dichloro-1 and ice acetic acid in the sodium dichromate. This synthesis
   method requires sodium dichromate as the reactant, polluting to the environment, it
15 needs longer reaction time, generally beyond eight hours, and the synthesis method is
   complicated. Therefore, it is necessary to propose a new synthesis method.

   8-naphthalic anhydride synthesis method, comprises the following steps:
         A: 1, 8-dimethyl-4, 5-dichloro-6-hydroxy naphthalene and 1.9L potassium
