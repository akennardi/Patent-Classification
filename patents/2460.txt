Description: 201800551
The Text Weaver is an innovative teaching and learning strategy designed to improve writing using a
reusable whiteboard style writing system.
The Text Weaver is a teaching and learning tool for improving writing. It is designed for hands on
learning. The Text Weaver utilises a whiteboard that requires the user to focus on key elements of
the writing processing as they complete the pre-printed whiteboard with their ideas during which
they refer to printed reminders and suggestions premeditated to ensure consistency in the user's
ongoing improvements in writing.
The Text Weaver whiteboard is designed to enhance the teaching/learning relationship between
students and their teacher.
A component of the Text Weaver, is the teacher guide and support manual/templates. The Text
Weaver is packaged in a whole class kit called the Write Box. The Write Box includes whiteboard
wall charts, a teacher manual, student workbook/whiteboard and marking pens.
