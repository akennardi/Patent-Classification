ABSTRACT
A solar-powered device for attracting and killing mosquito is of approximately the
size of a table-top lamp. The device comprises a lamp for attracting insects and a fan
for blowing attracted insects into the water stored at the base of the device.

This solar powered mosquito killer is approx. the size of a tabletop lamp. It will attract insects
(specifically mosquitos) by a special lamp and a fan will then blow them into the water below in the
base of the unit where they will drown. During the day the unit will be placed in sunlight which will
recharge the unit's internal batteries.
