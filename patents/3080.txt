Abstract
The invention relates to a wraparound sheet that is fixed by a slot with a barb edges to stop
it coming undone. It can be used to secure a labelling or information by being wrapped around
different commodities e.g. bottle necks. It is locked in position by one end going in a slot in itself and
locked in by a barb and can be made in any size and shape.
