         GENERAL BACKGROUND
         2-amino-benzothiazole is mainly used for medicine, dye intermediates, insoluble
   in water, when 4- or 6-position have substituents, it can make dye darker, for the
10 preparation of azo-type disperse dyes and miscellaneous ring type cationic dye,
   however, most of the existing synthetic methods using xylidene as the reactant, it is
   complicated and the final yield is not very high. Therefore, it is necessary to propose a
   new synthetic method for further improving the quality and yield of the product and
   reducing the byproduct content, it has important economic significance.
15

         Embodiment 1
         3 mol aniline, 12 L isopropanol solution with a mass fraction of 30% was added
   into the reaction vessel, the stirring speed was controlled at 110 rpm, 1.6 L
   hydrochloric     acid   solution   with  a   mass    fraction    of  10%    and   4   mol
20 2-methylthiocyanobenzene were added, the temperature of the solution was increased
   to 60 C, the stirring speed was controlled at 130 rpm, the temperature was kept at
   90   C, the temperature of the solution was reduced to 40 C and refluxed for 60 min,

                                                  3
   stand for cooling, crystal precipitated, the solid precipitate was dissolved in
   cyclohexane solution with a mass fraction of 50%, 60 kPa vacuum distillation,
   collected fractions of 80 'C, added 8 L oxalic acid solution with a mass fraction of
   20%, reducing the solution temperature to 10 'C, precipitated crystal, filter it, washed
 5 with the crystal with triethylamine solution with a mass fraction of 60%, and with
   ethylene glycol dimethyl ether solution with mass fraction of 80 %, dehydrated with
   anhydrous     sodium     sulfate  dehydration,     drying     at   50  'C, finally    get
   2-aminobenzothiazole 400.5g, yield 89%.
10       Embodiment 2

   2-methylthiocyanobenzene were added, the temperature of the solution was controlled
15 to 63 'C, the stirring speed was controlled at 140 rpm, the temperature was kept at 110
   rpm, the temperature of the solution was reduced to 42 'C and refluxed for 70 min,
   stand for cooling, precipitated crystal, the solid precipitate was dissolved in 53%
   cyclohexane solution, 63 kPa vacuum distillation, collected fractions of 85 'C, added
   8L oxalic acid solution with a mass fraction of 23%, reducing the solution temperature
20 to 13 'C, precipitated crystal, the crystal was filtered and the crystal was washed with
   triethylamine solution with a mass fraction of 63%, and washed with ethylene glycol
   dimethyl ether solution with a mass fraction of 82%, dehydrated with anhydrous
   potassium carbonate dehydration, drying at 55    0  C, obtained 409.5g, yield 91 %.
