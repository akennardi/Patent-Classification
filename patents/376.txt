          GENERAL BACKGROUND
          However, most of the existing synthetic methods are using 2-ethyl hexanol as the
   reactant, it is complicated and the final yield is not very high. Therefore, it is necessary
10 to propose a new synthetic method for further improving the quality and yield of the
   product and reducing the byproduct content, it has important economic significance.

          The purpose of the present invention is to provide drugs intermediates
