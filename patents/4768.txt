         Abstract
         Drugs intermediates 1,4-dibromo-2,3-butanediol synthesis method, comprises the
   following steps: the reaction vessel was added 2 mol 1,4-diaminobutanedione, 4-6
 5 mol 2-pyrrolidone, the solution temperature was reduced to 5-7 'C, then followed by
   the addition of 2-3 mol basic bismuth carbonate and 4-6 mol hydrogen bromide
   solution for 30-60 min, continued to reduce the temperature to 1-3 'C, controlled the
   stirring speed at 170-190 rpm, kept for 40-60 min, standing for 30-50 min,
   precipitated the solids, the filtrate was washed with butyric acid methyl ester solution
10 and the nitrile solution, recrystallized from the cyclohexanone solution to precipitate
   the crystals and the dehydrated with dehydrating agent, obtained the finished product
   1,4-dibromo-2,3-butanediol.

        GENERAL BACKGROUND
         1,4-dibromo-2,3-butanediol is mainly used as a drug intermediate. However, most
   of the existing synthetic methods are using dibromobutene as the reactant, it is
10 complicated and the final yield is not very high. Therefore, it is necessary to propose a
   new synthetic method for further improving the quality and yield of the product and
   reducing the byproduct content, it has important economic significance.
