                                                   Abstract
Poncho hooded towel with front pocket made of quick dry microfiber toweling material.
Special feature to this product is that it has a rectangle pocket on the front of the poncho
hooded towel. The entire towel then folds op into the one pocket and close up with Velcro.
Handles are also inserted in the inside of the front pocket that then can be used to carry the
towel in one bag. Ones swimmers can be placed into the pocket after it is folded up after
swimming to carry in the one bag.
This product will be made in various sizes to fit all ages children an all sizes adults.

CLAIMS:
Hooded microfiber poncho towel with rectangle front pocket.
Entire poncho made of quick dry microfiber material.
Front pocket made of double microfiber toweling material inside and out. It is then turned inside out
to fold in the entire hooded towel into one pocket/bag.
On both the middle top inside of both sides of the front pocket a strip of Velcro is inserted to enable
the bag to close up after the entire towel is folded into the pocket/bag.
There is also a blank plain white strip of material inserted on the top inside of the pocket on which
one can write your name on.
Inside the front pocket there is also a handle inserted to be able to carry the bag with a handle.
This microfiber hooded poncho towel will be made to fit all sizes children and adults.
